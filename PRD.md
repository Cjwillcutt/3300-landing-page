# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement  
Chris Willcutt is a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa graduating in May 2026, targeting data analyst and business analyst roles. His landing page allows him to showcase his analytics projects, technical skills, and real data work in more depth than a resume, helping recruiters quickly see how he applies data to solve business problems and making it easier for them to evaluate and contact him.

## 02 — Target Audience  
The target audience is recruiters and hiring managers at corporate and consulting companies hiring for data analyst or business analyst roles. Chris is primarily targeting opportunities in Chicago or hybrid roles in the Midwest, where employers are looking for candidates with strong technical skills and the ability to apply data to real business problems.

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics  

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|------|------------------|
| Increase visibility to recruiters | 50–100 website visits within 30–60 days |
| Generate recruiter interest | 5–10 recruiter or hiring manager messages |
| Support job applications | 10–20 applications that include the site |
| Secure interviews | 2–4 interviews from submitted applications |
